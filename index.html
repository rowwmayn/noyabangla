ORIGIN

The Experiment

This is Ramen & Sushi's journey.

We began with a simple question: What happens when the chaotic vibrancy of Bengali traditional patterns collides with the rigid structure of Western silhouettes?

"Threads of Tradition. Cuts of Modernity."

We don't just print patterns. We deconstruct the Jamdani weave, we re-engineer the Gamcha check, and we fuse them onto streetwear that speaks a global language with a Bengali accent.
